Hi!

Let's just start with the "about", shall we? I am a web developer based in Atlanta, Georgia.

I am a student at the full-stack Georgia Tech Coding Boot Camp and also a Junior at the University of Maryland University College majoring in digital media and web technology, and minoring in computer science. My favorite stack right now is React.js on the frontend and Node.js on the backend. I have experience working with Vue.js, Angular.js, MongoDB, CSS, MySQL, Express, jQuery, APIs, JSON, and AJAX.

The realization of my love for coding grew after many months of volunteer service and while studying Japanese abroad in Tokyo. I figured if I love spoken languages so much, why not give computer languages a chance? I haven't looked back since! The challenges coding brings are truly addictive and I look forward to continuously advancing my skills.
